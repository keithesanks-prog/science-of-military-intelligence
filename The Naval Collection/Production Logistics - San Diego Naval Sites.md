# Production Logistics: San Diego Naval Sites

## Locations

### 1. Naval Base San Diego (32nd Street)
- **Primary Focus:** Arleigh Burke-class Destroyers (DDG) and Ticonderoga-class Cruisers (CG).
- **Target Systems:** AN/SPY-1D (Phased Array), AN/SPS-67 (Surface Search).
- **Recording Goal:** Capture the "Aegis" aesthetic and the sheer scale of the SPY-1 plates.

### 2. Point Loma (Submarine Base & NAVWAR)
- **Primary Focus:** SIGINT infrastructure and Submarine radar/sonar masts.
- **Target Systems:** Intelligence collection nodes, dish arrays.
- **Recording Goal:** Contextualizing "The Waterfall Signal" with real-world intelligence centers in the background.

## Coordination Requirements

### Public Affairs Officer (PAO) Protocol
- **Permission Status:** Pending (Work in progress).
- **Escort Requirements:** 100% escort required for all pierside recording.
- **Equipment Restrictions:** No recording into sensitive "open hatches" or specific classified mast details (as per standard OPSEC).
- **Review Loop:** All footage must be reviewed by the escorting PAO before leaving the installation.

### Onsite Production Kit
- **Camera:** 4K Mobile rig with polarized filters (to reduce glare on naval paint).
- **Audio:** Directional lavalier for narration (accounting for pier noise/wind).
- **Reference:** Carry the "SMI-Veritasium" style guide for framing shots that allow for technical overlays.

## Safety & Access
- **PPE:** Steel-toe boots, hard hat, and life jacket for pier work.
- **Identification:** CAC card/Dependent ID + Media credentials.
