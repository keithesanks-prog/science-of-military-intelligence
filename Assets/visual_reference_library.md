## Arc 3 & 4: Secrets & Spies

### 7. [SMI_IN_THE_THING] The Great Seal Bug (The Thing)
- **Primary Innovator**: [SMI_IV_THEREMIN] Leon Theremin
- **Technical Specs**: Passive Resonant Cavity (Endovibrator). Resonant frequency ~800MHz - 1.8GHz. Mushroom-shaped tuning post inside a cylindrical silver-plated copper cavity.
- **Visual Reference**: Hidden inside the "beak" of the wooden Great Seal. Cutaway shows the 19mm metal cylinder, the 9-inch wire antenna, and the vibrating silver-plated diaphragm.
- **SMI Style**: Ghostly X-ray view with acoustic vibrations visualized as ripples hitting the "Return Signal."

### 8. [SMI_ID_ISAR] ISAR Ship Imaging
- **Technical Specs**: Inverse Synthetic Aperture Radar. Uses the ship's rotational motion (pitch/roll) to generate a high-resolution 2D map of scattering centers.
- **Visual Reference**: A frigate or destroyer shown not as a solid object, but as a "Point Cloud" of brilliant white-hot stars against a dark radar-blue background. Different colors represent computed energy intensities.
- **SMI Style**: "SMI-Veritasium" aesthetic: High-fidelity technical points with hud overlays for "Doppler History" and "Synthetic Aperture."

### 9. Operation Ivy Bells Pod (Induction Tap)
- **Technical Specs**: 20-foot long induction clamp. Magnetic induction capture (no physical contact).
- **Visual Reference**: A massive metal "clamshell" surrounding a thick, barnacle-covered undersea cable. 
- **SMI Style**: Deep-sea blue aesthetic with glowing magnetic induction loops showing "Signal Extraction."

### 10. Local Oscillator Leakage (Operation RAFTER)
- **Technical Specs**: Unintentional RF emissions from a superheterodyne receiver's Local Oscillator (LO) to the antenna.
- **Visual Reference**: A 1950s radio set with a faint, pulsing blue "ghost" radiation emanating from its antenna. An aircraft (Vickers Varsity) passes overhead with a sensor cone "catching" the leak.
- **SMI Style**: Technical diagram with "TEMPEST" labels and emanation cones.
