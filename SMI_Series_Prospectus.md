# Science of Military Intelligence: Series Prospectus

## Core Mission
To bridge the gap between fundamental physics, military history, and the highly classified world of intelligence operations. **SMI** focuses on the "Engineering of the Secret"—explaining not just what happened, but the scientific and procedural mechanisms that made it possible (or led to its failure).

## Target Audience
- **Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)** looking to contribute technical accuracy.
- **Industry Sponsors** in Defense, Aerospace, and Cloud Infrastructure.
- **Academic Institutions** looking for engaging engineering-led history.
- **The Curious Public** interested in high-fidelity technical storytelling.

## Episode Roadmap

### Arc 1: The Electromagnetic Foundation
- **Ep 1: The Spark That Changed the World**
    - The physics of Maxwell and Hertz; how a spark in a lab became the foundation of every sensor on the planet.
- **Ep 1.1: The Anatomy of the Pulse**
    - The technical "Block Diagram" of radar: From Master Oscillator to the Receiver's Noise Floor.
- **Ep 1.2: The Armor-Piercing Ghost**
    - Röntgen’s Accident. How the search for radio waves led to X-rays and Gamma rays—turning walls into windows for submarine hull checks and nuclear detection.
- **Ep 2: Friend or Foe**
    - The invention of radar and the "Digital Handshake" (IFF). How we distinguish a blip from a threat.
- **Ep 1.8: The Shape of the Signal**
    - The physics of resonance and polarization; how we match the metal to the math.
- **Ep 1.9: The Geometry of the Ghost**
    - Waveguides, diffraction, and side-lobes. How we "plumb" the energy and the stealth risks of leaked signals.
- **Ep 1.7: The Sound of Shadows**
    - The physics of Sonar. Moving from the "Radio Eye" to the "Acoustic Ear." Piezoelectric crystals and the SOFAR channel.
- **Ep 2.5: The Digital Lighthouse**
    - The legal mandate of AIS and maritime radar. How safety signals become a global tracking map for satellites.

### Arc 2: The Invisible War (Electronic Warfare & Stealth)
- **Ep 3: The Invisibility Paradox**
    - Why stealth is a "Shape" before it is a "Paint." How a single metal bucket can ruin a billion-dollar ghost.
- **Ep 6: Digital Mirage**
    - The science of Electronic Warfare: Resonant chaff, active decoys (Nulka), and the "fingerprint" of a radar transmitter (HULTEC).
- **Ep 8: The Heat Signature**
    - The physics of Infrared. How MTV flares and thermal veils block the seeker's eye.
- **Ep 15.5: The Microwave Ghost**
    - Asymmetric warfare: How household microwaves and simple engineering can defeat high-tech radar-seeking missiles.

### Arc 3: The Architecture of Information
- **Ep 4: The Mathematical Vault**
    - Cryptography as the "Armor of Numbers." The engineering of the Enigma and the birth of digital secrecy.
- **Ep 9.5: The Taxonomy of Secrets**
    - The "Engineering of Secrecy": How information is labeled (TS/SCI/SAP), the lifelong contract of the SF-312, and the SCIF as a Faraday cage.
- **Ep 9.7: The Trigger and the Code**
    - Who has the "Right to Fire"? The engineering of weapon permissions and the PAL (Permissive Action Link) logic.
- **Ep 17: The Cloud Empire**
    - The AWS Revolution. How private fiber-optic "Hyperlanes" replaced the public internet and changed global data collection forever.

### Arc 4: The Human & Strategic Signal
- **Ep 13: The Price of Betrayal**
    - The history of the "Mole" (Ames, Hanssen, Snowden). When the human component of the taxonomy fails.
- **Ep 16: Strategic Intel: The Decider**
    - Case studies in intelligence value: How specific intercepts (Midway, Cuban Missile Crisis) changed the course of world history.
- **Ep 18: The Life of a Data Point**
    - The transformation from raw signal to actionable intelligence. Explaining the focus on "Threat Markers" rather than mundane surveillance.
- **Ep 16.7: The Untouchables**
    - The Engineering of Ethics: How No-Strike Lists and targeting software enforce the rules of war.
- **Ep 16.8: The Phantom Signal**
    - Debunking the Ghost Hunter. Using TSCM (Technical Surveillance Countermeasures) and SIGINT logic to explain EMF spikes, "Spirit Box" aliasing, and the physics of thermal cold spots.

### Arc 5: Technical Collection "Greatest Hits"
- **Ep 14: Greatest Hits of Technical Collection**
    - Impossible engineering feats: The Great Seal Bug, Project Azorian (Submarine recovery), and Operation Ivy Bells (Undersea taps).
- **Ep 7.2: The Sky as a Mirror**
    - Atmospheric propagation: How the Ionosphere acts as a "Radio Mirror" and how solar storms rewrite the rules.
- **Ep 15: Tactical Recon**
    - The evolution of "Seeing": From Civil War balloons to the hyper-resolution of the SR-71 and Global Hawk drones.
